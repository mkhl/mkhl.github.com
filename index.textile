h2. About Martin

p<>. Martin Kühl is a software developer from Germany with an interest in computer languages and development tools. He is currently a student of informatics at the "University of Bremen":uni-bremen specializing in formal methods of software development and working on his thesis project.

p<>. He enjoys using--and developing on--both Linux and Mac OS X, learning about and using interesting technologies, and searching for the Quality Without a Name.

p<>. Martin believes that less is more, that worse is better, and that code must speak for itself.

p<>. His favourite monospaced fonts are "Terminus":terminus and "Inconsolata":inconsolata.
