h2. About

p. Martin Kühl is a software developer from Germany with an interest in computer languages and development tools. He is currently a student of informatics at the "University of Bremen":uni-bremen specializing in formal methods of software development and working on his thesis project.

[uni-bremen]http://www.informatik.uni-bremen.de/

p. He enjoys using -- and developing on -- both Linux and Mac OS X, learning about and applying interesting technologies, and searching for the "Quality Without a Name":qwan. His favourite monospaced fonts are "Terminus":terminus and "Inconsolata":inconsolata.  He currently writes code in either "TextMate":textmate or "GNU Emacs":emacs, manages versioning with "Git":git, and enters commands into the "Z shell":zsh.

[silver-bullet]http://www.virtualschool.edu/mon/SoftwareEngineering/BrooksNoSilverBullet.html
